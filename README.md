# 3 years of Black MIDI work.
Salutations, BLEEDING EDGE MONOTONE here! I was bored and realized that I don't have a public archive of my MIDIs out, so I figured today I'd get them scraped together and release them as a big ass file for all of you! Included in this repository is the following:
## What's included:
- Series I, II and III of my current released works as of 2/21/2026.
- The special AUDIO variants of each composition (Excluding FIVE PEBBLES as I lost the FLP...)
- THREE unreleased compositions: JUDGEMENT DAY and MOURNFUL CAMARADERIE, along with UVB-76 THE BUZZER!
- And most importantly the FLPs of each composition that were used!
## Special notes:
PLEASE NOTE: The FLPs use FL Studio versions 24.1 to 25 and may not open correctly on older versions of FL Studio. If you modify the songs, reupload them, or archive them, please give credit where available - I worked hard on these! In addition, some FLPs are lost (Namely FIVE PEBBLES, NO SIGNIFICANT HARASSMENT and a couple of others...) so if I find them I'll release them separately.
## And a little message from me:
Thank you all for everything, it's been wonderful and I look forward to the future in the BMC/BMCH where I can keep producing for you guys. Keep your eyes peeled for ARECIBO: INTERSTELLAR INSURGENCY, an animated short film, coming soon!
